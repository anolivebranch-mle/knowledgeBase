# Moderation

## About

Moderation is responsible for upholding the rules and values of Minor League Esports. The team enforces the [moderation guidelines](https://mlesports.gg/mod-guide/) and play a critical role in ensuring MLE remains an inclusive and non-toxic community. Moderation operates in all MLE discord servers as well as official match broadcasts. The mod team votes on every case and decision before any actions are taken (with the exception of issuing a server timeout if a member is actively breaking the rules and does not stop when instructed to by moderation) to ensure a fair enforcement of the rules.
